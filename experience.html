Business Class

Baggage Information

Pack What You Need

We want your journey to be seamless from the moment you pack. Here's everything you need to know about baggage on Skylink.

Economy Allowance

1 carry-on (7kg) + 1 checked bag (23kg) included on all Economy fares.

Business Allowance

2 carry-ons (7kg each) + 2 checked bags (32kg each) with priority handling.

Special Items

Sports equipment, musical instruments and medical devices handled with care.

Lost Baggage

24/7 baggage tracing service with compensation policy for delayed items.
